My mind

Representation of my mind when I close my eyes

In my home

I love being at home, specifically in my room, because it's quiet. It is the only place where I feel safe and my mind is clear.

In the school

I like to study, but I find it hard to think with so many people in such a small space. The teachers understand me most of the time and are good to me. But I don't have many friends because I don't understand them.

In public places

It's horrible for me to be in a place full of people, I feel like I'm drowning, my legs are shaking, but I still move them and try to stay calm.
